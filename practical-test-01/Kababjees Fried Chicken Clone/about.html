About Kababjees

Kababjees has been serving delicious fried chicken since 1998. What started as a small family restaurant has now grown into one of the most loved fried chicken chains in the country.

Our secret recipe has been perfected over the years, combining a special blend of herbs and spices that make our chicken truly unique. Every piece is hand-breaded and cooked to perfection, ensuring that crispy exterior and juicy interior that our customers love.

At Kababjees, we believe in using only the highest quality ingredients. Our chicken is sourced from local farms, and we prepare everything fresh daily. We're committed to providing our customers with the best food and service possible.
